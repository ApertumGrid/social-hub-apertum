Revenue Streams

Four ways your Hub makes money

Unlike other platforms where only the platform profits from activity, SocialHub cuts you in on everything that happens inside your Hub.

Stream 01
📡

Guest Broadcast Fees

Every time a guest posts inside your Hub, you automatically receive 50% of their broadcast fee. No action required — it hits your balance the moment they publish. Your own posts pay the standard fee to the platform, which covers storage and infrastructure.

Guest Text Post (1.2 ★) +0.6 ★ to you
Guest Image Post (3.0 ★) +1.5 ★ to you
Guest Video Post (10 ★) +5.0 ★ to you
Your own posts in your Hub Normal fee → 100% Platform
Stream 02
❤️

Landlord Interaction Split

When anyone likes or comments on a guest's post in your Hub, you earn 40% as Landlord Earnings. Every interaction in your space pays you rent.

Like or comment on guest post +0.4 ★ to you
Guest creator also earns 0.4 ★ (40%)
Platform keeps 0.2 ★ (20%)
Like on YOUR own post +0.8 ★ to you

🔒

Monthly Subscriptions

Set your own monthly price anywhere from 10★ to 500★ (~$0.10–$5). Lock exclusive posts behind the paywall. You keep 90% of every payment — paid in APTM when you withdraw.

Your cut per subscriber 90% of price
You set the price 10★ – 500★ / mo
Subscribers-only posts Blurred for non-subs
Platform keeps 10% only

👥

Hub Follows

Following your Hub costs 1 ★. You receive 90% (0.9 ★) the instant someone taps Follow — and they stay in your orbit forever via their Following feed.

Per follow received +0.9 ★ to you

Hub Owner Tools

Everything you need to run your community

Hub owners get a full suite of tools to moderate, customise, and grow their space.

📊

Live Analytics Panel

Stars earned, follower count, total posts, active subscribers, top posts by engagement, and a 30-day follower growth chart — all in one place.

📌

Pin a Post

Lock your most important post to the top of the feed permanently. Pinned posts show a gold 📌 Pinned badge and always appear first.

✕

Content Moderation

Remove any guest post that doesn't fit your community with one tap. You control your space — no approval needed from the platform.

🛡

Co-Moderators

Assign trusted members as co-mods by wallet address. They get moderation rights in your Hub so you don't have to be online 24/7.

📋

Hub Rules

Write your community rules and they appear at the top of your Hub for every visitor. Set the tone, reduce friction, keep quality high.

🔔

Follower Notifications

Every time you broadcast a new post, all your Hub followers receive an automatic notification — bringing them straight back to your content.

⏰

Subscription Renewal Alerts

Subscribers get a bell notification 3 days before their subscription expires — so they renew instead of lapsing. More recurring revenue for you.

★

Subscriber Badges

Active subscribers get a purple ★ Sub badge next to their name in your feed — social proof that drives more sign-ups.

🔒

Subscriber-Only Posts

Mark any post as Subscribers Only. Non-subscribers see a blurred preview with a lock overlay — a powerful conversion tool that sells itself.
